## Features

<ul>
  <li>Supports OHLC, candlestick, line, area, bar graphs.</li>
  <li>Supports markers over graphs and axes, including labels, lines, arrows, callouts, shapes, etc.</li>
  <li>Easy to extend to add custom graph types and markers.</li>
  <li>Resizable multiple panels and multiple graphs and axes in single panel.</li>
  <li>Show tooltips and crosshair lines for data points.</li>
  <li>Zoom and pan of axes and graph. double tap axes to reset zoom.</li>
  <li>Supports momentum scrolling.</li>
  <li>Show animation when zooming.</li>
  <li>Dynamic data loading with loading indicator.</li>
  <li>Live update of data and graphs.</li>
  <li>Customizable theme support with dark and light presets.</li>
</ul>
