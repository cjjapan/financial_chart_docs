Base class for all graphs.  

## Properties

### `layer`
**type:** `int`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `0`  

The layer of the graph. higher layers are drawn on top of lower layers.

### `visible`  
**type:** `bool`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `true`  

Whether the graph is visible or not. If false, the graph will not be drawn. 

### `highlighted`  
**type:** `bool`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `false`  


### `selected`
**type:** `bool`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `false`  


### `valueViewPortId`
**type:** `String?`  
**required:** `false`   
**default:** `""`  


### `hitTestMode`  
**type:** `GHitTestMode`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `GHitTestMode.auto`  


### `crosshairHighlightValueKeys`  
**type:** `List<String>`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `[]`  


### `overlayMarkers`  
**type:** `List<GMarker>`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `[]`  


### `theme` 
**type:** `GGraphAreaTheme`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `GGraphAreaTheme()`  

### `render`
**type:** `GGraphAreaRender?`  
**required:** `false`  
**default:** `GGraphAreaRender()`
